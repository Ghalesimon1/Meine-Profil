PLAN HIMALAYA

Plan Himalaya is a Nepalese agency specialised in trips and treks through Nepal, Sikkim & Darjeeling, as well as Bhutan and Tibet.

We guide your steps trekking and travelling in the Himalayas of Nepal, Sikkim & Darjeeling, Bhutan, Tibet.

WE CREATE

As we are locals, we know our country. We create our own itineraries based on our experience, our tastes and our philosophy as an agency, and we renew them in order to improve them.

WE PERSONALISE

We have quite a few published itineraries, but there are more and they can all be customised. Tell us what you want, what you like, and together with you we will create a tailor-made itinerary for you.

NEAR YOU

One of our advantages is that we deal directly with you, we are travellers and trekkers, and we have the same way of thinking. Being Nepalese, but having Jordi with us brings the best of both sides.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Our entire income is invested locally.We support social and educational projects in Nepal, we do not offer elephant-back safaris and we do not offer any activities that we consider unethical.

ABOUT US

Plan Himalaya is a Nepalese agency specialised in trips and treks through Nepal, Sikkim & Darjeeling, as well as Bhutan and Tibet. We are a small agency and we love close contact with you. On the ground, we follow your trip day by day… We create our own itineraries, always giving them a different touch and we can personalize them for you. As a work philosophy, we love to give sense and logic to our itineraries and we take care of those small details that make the travel experience a little different. Because we believe that travel means experience.
